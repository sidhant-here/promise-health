How a visit works

From booking to your care plan, in four steps

Book your visit

Choose a doctor, a reason for the visit, and a time — online in under two minutes, or by phone.

Check in

Arrive 10 minutes early, or check in from your phone before you get here to skip the front desk.

See your doctor

Unhurried visits — your doctor has your full history pulled up before you sit down.

Get your plan

Notes, prescriptions, and next steps land in your patient portal before you're back in the car.
